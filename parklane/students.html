Instructor rules & regulations
By accessing the online learning portal, you agree to abide by these rules and regulations. Violations may result in sanctions as per the institution's policies. These rules are in place to ensure a safe and productive online learning environment for all users.
  • Be respectful and considerate in all online interactions with instructors and fellow students.
  • Avoid offensive or disruptive behavior, including harassment or hate speech.
  • Do not copy or distribute course content without authorization.
  • Maintain academic integrity and avoid plagiarism or cheating in all assessments.
