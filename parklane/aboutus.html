Creche (Infant Care):
In our creche program, we provide a caring and nurturing environment for infants aged six weeks to 18 months. Our experienced caregivers prioritize the individual needs of each child, offering personalized attention and support as they explore and learn through sensory experiences, music, and gentle interactions. Our focus is on creating a safe and stimulating environment where infants can develop foundational skills and form secure attachments.

Nursery (Toddler Care):
Our nursery program is tailored for toddlers aged 18 months to three years old. At this stage, children are curious explorers, eager to discover the world around them. Through hands-on activities, play-based learning, and guided exploration, we encourage their natural curiosity and support their emerging language, motor, and social skills. Our nurturing environment fosters independence, confidence, and a love for learning, laying the groundwork for future academic success.
Kindergarten (Pre-School):
In our kindergarten program, we provide a dynamic and enriching environment for children aged three to six years old. Following the Montessori philosophy, our curriculum is designed to promote independence, creativity, and critical thinking. Students engage in purposeful activities that span across practical life, sensorial exploration, language development, mathematics, cultural studies, and the arts. Our qualified Montessori teachers guide each child's individual learning journey, fostering a sense of wonder and joy in discovery.
